    How We Started

    Heading back to more than a decade ago, there were really few shops which used to sell branded musical instruments.
    The idea for Guitar Shop originated in 2008 with the aim to provide musical instruments of quality brands to the Nepali
    community at a reasonable price and the first outlet of Guitar Shop was opened in Ason. Guitar Shop now has a total of 8
    outlets all over Nepal and houses some of the leading music-instrument brands. Guitar Shop is more than just a guitar store
    where we offer the largest selection of popular guitars, amplifiers, keyboards, drums, percussion, microphones,
    studio gear and more for your musical journey no matter your musical taste.



    About The Journey

    We offer products of brands such as Alesis, Paiste, Blackstar, Mantra, Joyo, Ernie Ball and Dream Maker with many of the brands not
    available in any other music stores of Nepal. However, we keep our prices affordable to make sure that our valuable clients will find all
    the best gear at the guaranteed lowest price. Well help you find the instrument which you could connect to, with no compromises in both quality
    and affordability.
    Our connection with the customers is not limited to the selling of instruments but also after the sales have been made.
    We provide instrument servicing, repairing and maintenance for optimum sound quality and long-life of the instrument.
    Also, our service departments have some of the most qualified instrument technicians who will surely convince you that all of your musical sales
    and repair needs can be fulfilled in one place and you dont need to compromise on anything less
